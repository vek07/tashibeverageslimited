Our Story

Tashi Beverages Limited (TBL) is a proudly Bhutanese company under the Tashi Group of Companies, engaged in the production of high-quality bottled water and carbonated soft drinks. Since its establishment in 2007, the company has remained committed to delivering refreshing, safe, and reliable beverages to consumers across Bhutan. TBL upholds international production standards, emphasizes sustainable practices, and contributes meaningfully to the nation's industrial and economic progress.


We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to ensure every product meets the highest standards of quality and taste. Our commitment to sustainability and community development drives everything we do.
